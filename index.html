    Make your SANE scanner more accessible. Have a little Sanity.

    Project maintained by alitheg Theme inspired by mattgraham

    The problem

    SANE: It's great for connecting your USB scanner to your Linux machine or brand-spanking new Raspberry Pi.

    What it's not so great for is sharing that scanner with others. "What about saned?", I hear you ask. Try sharing a SANE scanner with a windows user and you'll see what's wrong with it.

    The solution? Sanity.

    Sanity is a web frontend for the scanimage binary. You check it out, start it up, and scan away. Previous scans are listed for your perusal and preview, and can be downloaded by anyone with access to your server.
